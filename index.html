Privacy Policy | Last Updated: April 10, 2026

1. Introduction

RidgeLine ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Camera Access

  • Purpose: To enable peer-to-peer video calls and location sharing features.
  • Collection: Only when you explicitly grant permission and use a camera-related feature.
  • Storage: Camera data is not stored on our servers; it is streamed directly between peers.

Location Data

  • Purpose: To provide location-based matching and mountain guide services.
  • Collection: Only when you enable location services in the app.
  • Storage: Location data is encrypted and associated with your account for service delivery.

Account Information

  • Purpose: To authenticate users and manage subscriptions.
  • Collection: Email, username, and payment information via RevenueCat.
  • Storage: Securely encrypted on our servers.

Analytics

  • Purpose: To understand app usage and improve service quality.
  • Collection: App crashes, feature usage, and session duration in anonymous form.
  • Storage: Aggregated and anonymized analytics only.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve RidgeLine services.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • To send service-related notifications.
  • To comply with legal obligations.
  • To respond to your support requests.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription and payment processing.
  • Service providers: Only to the extent necessary to provide services.
  • Legal authorities: If required by law.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active, then removed within 30 days after deletion.
  • Camera/location streams: Not stored; transmitted only during live sessions.
  • Analytics: Retained for 12 months in anonymized form.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data.
  • Request correction or deletion of your information.
  • Opt-out of analytics collection.
  • Withdraw location or camera permissions at any time.

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure. Avoid transmitting sensitive information you are not comfortable sharing.

8. Third-Party Links

RidgeLine may include links to third-party services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies separately.

9. Children's Privacy

RidgeLine is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated through in-app notification or email.
